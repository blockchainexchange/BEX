Frequently Asked Questions:

+
What is Blockchain Exchange?

Using the Ether Delta smart contract and Fork Delta order book source code, Blockchain Exchange is a semi-decentralized exchange for ERC20 tokens use off-chain order book with the on-chain settlement. The majority of the operation will handle direct on the Blockchain thereby traders never has to go for the ownership for their tokens, enabling the whole system to deal with money transfers and execute trades in a trustless manner.

+
What is a decentralized exchange?

Unlike centralized exchange decentralized exchange does not depend on any central authority to store user’s fund and facilitate trade. In the case of Blockchain Exchange, funds are stored in a smart contract hosted on the Ethereum network. Trade will occur between user’s wallet and the smart contract.

+
What are the some notable features of the Blockchain Exchange?

  • Blockchain Exchange is an open source decentralized exchange, fair community of developers who are easily accessed and open source codebase.
  • Blockchain Exchange welcomes any feedback, criticism, ideas, and help that the community would like to give.
  • Blockchain Exchange does not and will not ever charge fees for token listings.
  • Blockchain Exchange also lists the most tokens of any exchange with new tokens added every day.

+
How to create an account on Blockchain Exchange?

Just click on the account which is at the top right of trading view and then click on new account. A new address and the private key will generate automatically.

+
How to deposit the funds?

It is very easy! Click on deposit which is at the top left of balance view, select the amount of either Ethereum or tokens you would like to deposit. After that you will be ask to sign or accept the transaction. Once the transaction has been sent, your fund will be deposited into the Ethereum smart contract securely. Then, you will be able to trade on Blockchain Exchange with those funds.

+
How to withdraw the funds?

Click on withdraw which is at the top left of balance view, select the amount of either Ethereum or tokens you would like to withdraw. After that you will be ask to sign or accept the transaction. Once the transaction has been sent, you can withdraw your fund securely into your Ethereum account.

+
How long does it take to withdraw or deposit the funds?

It entirely depends on speed of the Ethereum network. In case if you would like to deposits or withdraw your funds quickly then you have to pay higher gas price for your transaction. Generally, in normal gas price withdraw or transfer does not take longer than 5 minutes. However, when the Ethereum network is clogged, transactions could be delayed.

+
How do I select a token?

In Blockchain Exchange, you can trade extensive number of tokens that are available on the Ethereum network. We have a dexterous team of professionals who list as many token as possible. You can found the listed tokens at the dropdown near the upper left section of Blockchain Exchange.

+
How do I place an order?

You can place an order once funds have been place in the smart contract. If you would like to create a new buy or sell order then find the new order section. Select the buy or sell option and enter the token amount you would like to buy or sell, the price, the expiration time, and hit buy/sell. After that you have to sign the transaction, so that it can be sent to the order book. Once a taker for your order has been found, Blockchain Exchange will use that sign order to execute a trade using the smart contract Ethereum network.

+
How do I take an order?

To take your existing order click on the order that you would like to take in the order book. After that a window will appear that will ask you to specify the amount you would like to take. Afterclicking on buy or sell,you will then be prompted to sign the transaction, so that your trade can be sent to the smart contract. Blockchain Exchange will give you a transaction ID and link. You can track your trade status by this link.

+
How do I cancel an order?

Go to My transactions section and click the orders tab. On order tab you will get your listed order with a link to cancel them. You will be asked to sign a transaction in order to cancel the order.

+
What fees are associated?

There are two fees associated with Blockchain Exchange. The first is the small Ethereum fee while signing the transactions to the Ethereum network and the second is a 0.3% on execution of orders.

+
How to change the default gas price?

It is very easy, you just need to click on the drop down at the top right of Blockchain Exchange (where you created your account) and click on “Gas Price”at the bottom of that menu. Then, you can set the gas price using Gwei denomination.

To cancel an order, take a look at the My Transactions section and click the Orders tab. Your active orders will be listed there with a link to cancel them. You will be asked to sign a transaction in order to cancel the order.
